The Question:

Don't you just hate hearing "I'm sorry, the ice cream machine is down right now?"

What if you could look and see which McDonald's locations other people have heard that same phrase from so that you could choose a better location to fulfill your ice cream cravings?

The Solution:

Lists the McDonald's Locations with Status

McIceCream will show you a list of locations, when they were last reported, and if the ice cream machines were good or "broken."

The application is automatically updated with the most recent reports of every McDonald's location in the database.

Missing location? No problem! You or anyone can request a location be added, and the database will automatically update for every one as soon as the request is completed.

See Details on Each McDonald's Locations

You can see specific details on every location in the database.

If you order ice cream, you can report to everyone whether you received it, or were told once again that the machine was "broken."

Every user that reports the results of their ice cream order is helping everyone else. Do your part!
